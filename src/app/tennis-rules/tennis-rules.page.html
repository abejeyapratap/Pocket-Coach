Tennis Rules Basic Rules
  • The ball must be within the boundary lines at all times and if a player hits the ball out of bounds, the opponent gets the point
    • If the ball touches the boundary line, that is still considered in bounds
  • Players cannot touch the net or cross to the other side of the net
  • Players cannot carry the ball or catch it with the racket
  • Players cannot hit the ball twice in a row
  • The ball must pass your side of the net before you can hit it
  • If the ball bounces twice on your side before you hit it, your opponent gets the points
  • Players must allow the serve to bounce first before returning it
  • The first server is determined by a coin toss
  • The server serves for the whole game and switches after each game
Scoring System
  • There are 4 points per game and they increment from Love(0)-15-30-40-Game
    • Everyone starts at Love(0)
    • Winner of the game is the one that reaches game
  • Players must win by at least two points
    • For example, if both players are at 40-40, you must score 2 consecutive points to win
  • The winner of the set is the first one to win 6 games with at least a 2 game lead
  • If a set is at 6-6, you play a tie-break game
    • In the tie-break match, the winner is the first to reach 7 points instead of 4 and must win by 2 points
  • A match is usually played as a best of 3 or best of 5 set
 Serving
  • Foot Fault - When the server steps into the court or crosses the center mark before they make contact the ball
  • Players must serve on the rights side of the court at the beginning of each game and switch after each point
  • The goal is to serve the ball into the box that is diagonal to them. The serve is good if the ball lands in that box which is called the service box
