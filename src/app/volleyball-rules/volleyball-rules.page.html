Volleyball Rules Basic Rules
  • Maximum of 6 players are allowed on court per team, 3 on the front row, 3 on the back row
  • Maximum of 3 hits per side
  • A player is not allowed to hit the ball twice in a roll
  • A ball hitting the boundary lines is "in"
  • It is illergal to catch, hold, oor throw the ball
  • It is legal to contact the ball with any part of the body
  • A ball is considered out if it hits
    • An antenna
    • The floor outside the boundary line
    • The ceiling in a non playable area
    • The referee stand or pole
  • If two or more player both contact the ball at the same time, it is considered as one touch and either player is allowed to contact the ball again
  • A player can not block or attack a serve from on or inside the 10 foot line
Rules Regulation
  • Result in a regulation is a point for the opponent
    • Stepping on or across the boundary line before you make contact with the ball (it is okay if you land on the inside as long as you jump behind the line
    • Contacting the ball illegally (catch, throw, hold)
    • Touching the net while ball is in play
    • Crossing the center courtline
    • Serving out of order
 Advanced Rules
  • A back row player blocking the ball (when the back row player is near the net and makes contact with the ball while part of their body is above the net)
  • Overlapping (out of rotation)
  • A back row player can not attack a ball if it is completely above the net while positioned on or in front of the attack line
