Volleyball Basic Rules Basic Rules
  • Maximum of 6 players are allowed on court per team, 3 on the front row, 3 on the back row
  • Maximum of 3 hits per side
  • A ball hitting the boundary lines is "in"
  • A ball is considered out if it hits
    • An antenna
    • The floor outside the boundary line
    • The referee stand or pole
  • Violations that will result in a point for the other team
    • Stepping on or across the boundary line before you make contact with the ball
    • Contacting the ball illegally (catch, throw, hold)
    • Touching the net while ball is in play
    • Crossing the center courtline
    • Serving out of order
